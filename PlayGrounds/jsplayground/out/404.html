What is JSPlayground.dev?

It is a web application that provides a minimal and fast JavaScript playground for free. You can think of it as a tiny editor for you to quickly test any JavaScript supported by your browser. It works pretty much as a REPL, where you are given instant feedback on the Console screen as you change your code. This makes it ideal for quick prototyping and experimentation without needing to launch another tool for the job.
